# Business-Health-Analyzer
Built an AI-powered Business Intelligence solution using Python, Machine Learning, Generative AI, and Power BI. Performed sales analysis, revenue forecasting, demand prediction, customer segmentation, business risk assessment, and AI-driven consulting with interactive dashboards for actionable insights.

# 🚀 AI-Powered Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Platform

## 📌 Project Overview

An end-to-end Business Intelligence solution that transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights using Data Analytics, Machine Learning, Generative AI, and Power BI.

The project follows a complete analytics pipeline:

📊 Descriptive Analytics → 🤖 Predictive Analytics → 🧠 Prescriptive Analytics → 📈 Business Intelligence

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze business performance across regions, products, and customers
- Forecast future revenue and product demand
- Segment customers based on purchasing behavior
- Predict business risk levels
- Generate AI-powered consulting reports
- Visualize insights through interactive Power BI dashboards
